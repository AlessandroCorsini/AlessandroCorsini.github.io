Alessandro Corsini

PhD student, Neuroscience | MSc, Computer Science and Engineering

I am a PhD student in Neuroscience at the Italian Institute of Technology (CTNSC@IIT), studying the nature of speech perception in the human brain with Prof. Luciano Fadiga and Alessandro D'Ausilio.

Featured Projects

  • ArticEncod_difficulty

    I use Partial Information Decomposition (PID) to assess articulatory encoding in EEG signals during speech listening.

  • PyPID_mvn

    I implemented Partial Information Decomposition in Python for continuous gaussian variables.

  • NMA_2023_MotorImagery

    A simple project exploring tongue and hand motor imagery with ECOG signals, developed during the NMA 2023 course.
