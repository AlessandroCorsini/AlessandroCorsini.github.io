About me

I am a PhD student in Translational Neuroscience and Neurotechnology at the Center of Translational Neurophysiology for Speech and Communication (CTNSC@Unife). I studied Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano both for my B.Sc. and for my M.Sc. During my M.Sc. I spent one year at the Technische Universität Belrin (TUB) thank to a double degree program, where I earned a M.Sc. in Innovation in Information and Communication Technology.

My research interests are in the field of the neurophysiology of speech perception and speech-BCI. I am now studying the role of the internal articulatory simulation in the listener's brain during speech perception.
